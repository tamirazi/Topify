Topify

Topify is a PWA built with Angular & Spotify’s web api. designed with a clean UI & an intuitive UX, the application displays the user’s top artists, tracks, albums & genres and suggests playlists based on them.

Privacy & Terms of use

Topify is open source and works as a client side application. it requires a Spotify account and a granted acsses to your Spotify information. Topify does not save or make any use of your data & information.
